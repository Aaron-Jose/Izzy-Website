About The Creator

Hey! I'm Izzy, the hands and heart behind The Local Rug Dealer. My journey into rug making started from a love for unique, expressive art that you can touch and feel. I was tired of generic, mass-produced decor and wanted to create pieces that tell a story and bring a space to life.

Each rug is a labor of love, meticulously crafted in my small studio. I pour my creativity and passion into every single piece, ensuring it's not just a rug, but a piece of art that's uniquely yours. Thanks for stopping by and supporting a local artist!
