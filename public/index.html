Hello, my name is Izzy and I am The Local Rug Dealer. I am a 19 year old student who is working towards paying off her debt in a non-traditional way! My small business is held in my bedroom with every product having the utmost amount of care in it.








STEP 1

I choose my design and select the coloured wool needed for my amazing design.

STEP 2

Then I prepare my fabric by stretching it across my hand made frame (shoutout to my dad steve for making it for me)

STEP 3

Next I use a projector to project my design onto my canvas and use markers to sketch it on.

STEP 4

Now the interesting step begins as I choose my starting wool and thread up my tufting gun and start tufting away.(Depending on the design this process can take a few hours).

STEP 5

Once the design is complete I then take it off the canvas, glue the back and shave and shape it.

STEP 6

Then some backing is fitted to the back and BOOM an awesome rug is hand crafted.
